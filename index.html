EZU have been a band since February 13th 2010, begins their first Demotape with titled "You'll Never Walk Alone" used to be an Original Film Soundtrack by title "Menebus Impian", in Jakarta Indonesia. EZU first stage debut when they perform at the Lounching Of Menebus Impian Film's at Jakarta Theatre, Jakarta Indonesia. The day was the first time the EZU song is played to the Public.

Previously, there was a long story as the beginning of EZU there. Several years before the founding of EZU, Julian could make music with the Ferry Project, Ulfie as well as some of their friends who then managed to release several songs for other band. But after a few months later they decided to part ways with the band. And after two years Julian, Ferry and Ulfie met again and agreed to build a band named EZU.

EZU taken from the name of a perennial lake in Japan, EZU in Nigerian Language means : "That's All You Give". That is the way that we choose to put the Spirit, Friendship, Nature, and Life as a part of our Lyrics Loudly, which can create the Brilliant Positif Idea without has any Polaric Emotion and more.
